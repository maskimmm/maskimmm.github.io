Design PV

Design PV is an application conceptualized by Abigail and developed by Hakim from December 2021 to January 2022 its main purpose is to analyze the potential power that can be generated by solar power based on solar irradiance modeling, to calculate the amount of capacity that can be installed in an area, and to measure the investment costs and benefits obtained from the solar cell installation plan.

INHES
